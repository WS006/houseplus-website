## Our Story: A Decade of Growth

Founded in 2012, House Plus Group began with a simple vision: to provide high-quality, affordable products directly from Chinese factories to buyers worldwide. What started as a small manufacturing operation has evolved into a comprehensive supply chain solution serving diverse industries and markets.

**Key Milestones:**

| Year | Achievement |
|------|-------------|
| 2012 | House Plus Group founded in China, focusing on consumer electronics |
| 2015 | Expanded to solar energy products, entering the African market |
| 2018 | Established House Plus Ltd office in Lagos, Nigeria (Ikeja GRA) |
| 2020 | Launched 3C electronics product line, serving 30+ countries |
| 2022 | Opened second Nigeria office at Herbert Macaulay St, Ikeja GRA |
| 2024 | Serving 50+ countries with 500+ product SKUs and 1000+ satisfied clients |

Our growth trajectory reflects our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Each milestone represents not just business expansion, but a deepening of our relationships with partners and customers across the globe.

## What We Do: Three Core Industries

House Plus Group operates across three interconnected industries, each with its own expertise and market presence:

### 1. Solar Energy Solutions

In an era of increasing energy demands and environmental consciousness, our solar energy division provides cutting-edge solutions including solar panels, inverters, lithium batteries, charge controllers, and solar street lights. We work with leading manufacturers to ensure every product meets international quality standards and delivers exceptional performance.

**Why Choose Our Solar Products:**
- Factory direct pricing without intermediaries
- ISO-certified manufacturing processes
- Comprehensive warranty and after-sales support
- Scalable solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial applications

### 2. Home Appliances

Our home appliances line includes air fryers, blenders, rice cookers, and other essential household products. We understand that quality appliances enhance daily life, which is why we partner with manufacturers who share our commitment to durability and functionality.

**Product Range:**
- Air fryers and cooking appliances
- Kitchen blenders and food processors
- Rice cookers and heating appliances
- Vacuum cleaners and cleaning equipment

### 3. 3C Electronics

The 3C (Computer, Communication, Consumer Electronics) sector is where innovation meets practicality. Our 3C product line includes smart watches, power banks, USB cables, Bluetooth speakers, and other consumer electronics that keep people connected and productive.

**Featured 3C Products:**
- Smart watches with health monitoring features
- High-capacity power banks for mobile devices
- Premium USB cables and charging solutions
- Wireless Bluetooth speakers and audio devices

## Our Competitive Advantages

### Factory Direct Pricing

By eliminating intermediaries, we pass significant savings directly to our customers. Our direct relationships with manufacturers ensure competitive pricing without compromising on quality.

We understand that many businesses need customized solutions. Our Original Equipment Manufacturing (OEM) and Original Design Manufacturing (ODM) services allow clients to create products tailored to their specific needs and branding requirements.

### Quality Assurance

Every product undergoes rigorous quality control processes. Our ISO-certified facilities and experienced quality teams ensure that products meet international standards and customer expectations.

### Comprehensive Support

From order placement to after-sales service, our dedicated team provides 24/7 support in multiple languages. We handle logistics, customs clearance, and delivery to ensure a seamless experience.
